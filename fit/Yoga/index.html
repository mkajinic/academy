Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India. Yoga means that which allows you to attain to your higher nature. The yogic system is a subtle manipulation of your system. Every mudra (hand gesture to direct prana through the body), karma or kriya, the very way of breathing,every asana is focused towards this.
