Great Gastronomic offers

Amazing offer of natural and healthy food, prepared traditionaly. The food offering in Osijek is off the charts. From hearty soups to roasted game to cured meats and tasty pastas, the common ingredients are homemade and fresh.

Wine Tastings

Visit Baranja wine cellars.Wine roads surround the small villages and traditional houses can still be visited where the wine is made and stored.

Culture Festivals
