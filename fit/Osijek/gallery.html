    Cathedral

    This is the tallest building in Croatia outside of Zagreb. The interior is a treasure trove of neo-Gothic ornamentation, with a succession of pinnacled altars overlooked by exuberant stained glass windows.

    Water Gates

    Water Gates in Old Town Tvrda, Of the fortification system, only the northern side of the walls now remain intact, as well as parts of the first and eighth bastions along with the northern gate known as the 'water gate'

    Park

    Osijek has many parks beautifully decorated and was nominated for Golden Flower of Europe

    Kopacki rit

    The greenest waters and forests-Kopački rit is one of the largest natural wetlands in Europe and as such has been recommended for inclusion in the UNESCO List of Natural Heritage. Large herds of deer are practically a trade mark of Kopački rit, despite the fact that herds of wild boar are just as large.

    Osjecko beer

    Osječko is the first Croatian beer ever produced, and has been produced since 1697.It is the oldest beer in the country. With its unique taste you can easily recognize it with its brown bottle

    Concert in Old Town
